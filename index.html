Optum is a health services company that is committed to making health care work better leading to better experiences, health, and lower costs for individuals. It was formed as a subsidiary of UnitedHealth Group in 2011 by merging existing pharmacy and care delivery services into the Optum brand, which comprises three main businesses: OptumHealth, OptumInsight, and OptumRx.
